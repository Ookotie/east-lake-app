## 4. FLOOD ZONE & EVACUATION

### Flood Zone
Wentworth is an **inland community** in unincorporated Pinellas County (East Lake corridor). Based on its inland location east of US-19:
- Most likely **FEMA Flood Zone X** (minimal flood hazard, outside 100-year floodplain)
- No mandatory flood insurance requirement expected
- The area does have golf course ponds nearby; localized drainage issues possible

### Evacuation Zone
Wentworth's inland East Lake location is generally in a **non-evacuation zone** or Zone E (highest -- minimal risk). East Lake corridor properties are typically NOT in mandatory evacuation zones A-D. However, verify at https://kyz.pinellas.gov/ by entering the address.

The Tarpon Springs shelter list includes **East Lake High School** as a hurricane shelter, which is very close to Wentworth.

**PASS.** The golf course lot is a dealbreaker per stated criteria. Even setting that aside, the pricing is aggressive for a quick flip, the square footage is below target, and the property has had 3 owners in 4 years which is unusual for a $1M+ home. If the buyer is flexible on golf course proximity, offer no more than $1,050,000-$1,100,000 based on comps and the Redfin estimate.
